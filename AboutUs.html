Our History:

Clarke's UK PLC was founded in 1872 by Edward Clarke, a visionary entrepreneur who sought to create a financial institution dedicated to serving individuals and businesses across the United Kingdom with integrity and reliability. From its humble beginnings in a small town branch in Yorkshire, Clarke's UK PLC grew steadily through decades of economic change, always staying focused on fostering long-term relationships with customers. Throughout the 20th century, Clarke's UK PLC expanded its services beyond traditional banking, introducing innovative solutions to meet the evolving needs of the modern economy. Today, we operate nationwide, with a network of branches, online banking services, and corporate solutions that support individuals, families, and businesses alike. Our commitment to stability, personalized service, and community support has been the cornerstone of our success, and we remain proud of our legacy of trust and excellence in the UK banking sector.

Our Mission:

At Clarke's UK PLC, our mission is to empower individuals, families, and businesses by providing secure, reliable, and innovative banking solutions that help them achieve financial success. We are dedicated to delivering a seamless and personalized banking experience, underpinned by a deep sense of responsibility to our customers, employees, and communities. We strive to lead the way in sustainable financial practices, using advanced technology and expert insights to guide our clients through an ever-changing financial landscape. With a strong foundation built on trust, integrity, and innovation, we are committed to ensuring that every customer’s financial journey is as smooth and rewarding as possible.
